Privacy Policy

QR Craft Share values your privacy. We collect and store only the data necessary to provide a secure, reliable, and personalized experience:

QR Content Data

When you create a QR code, its content (e.g., Wi-Fi credentials, links, vCards) is saved locally on your device and securely synced to our servers.

This ensures your QR codes are backed up and available across your devices.

Account Information

When you sign up or log in, we collect your email address to identify your account and enable features like cloud sync and restoring your QR library.

Emails are stored securely on our servers and are never shared with third parties.

Data Linking

QR data saved on our servers is linked to your account email for secure storage and synchronization.

This linkage allows you to access your QR codes from any device after logging in.

No Tracking or Profiling

We do not collect data for advertising, tracking, or profiling purposes.

Your QR content and account information are never sold or shared externally.

Data Sharing

Your data is used only to deliver app features (e.g., cloud sync) and is never transferred or sold to third parties.

Security

All data on our servers is encrypted and stored securely.

We follow industry best practices to protect your data both on-device and in the cloud.

Your Control

You can delete QR codes locally and request permanent deletion from our servers at any time.

For account deletion or data inquiries, please use the account deletion page or contact help@qrcraftshare.app.

We are committed to transparency and keeping your data secure. If you have any questions, reach out — we’re here to help.
